About Me

Hi, I’m Vikram — someone who genuinely believes that the best conversations in life often happen over street food in a new city.

I’m originally from the Pink City of Rajasthan, India, and at 29, I’ve developed a strong love for traveling and exploring places through their local flavors. For me, every city has a story — and that curiosity to explore has shaped not just my personal life, but my professional journey as well.

My career started back in 2017 at Teleperformance, where I stepped in as an Executive. It was my first real exposure to the corporate world — fast-paced, people-driven, and full of learning. That experience built my foundation in communication and understanding people.

But I’ve always been someone who likes to experiment and evolve, which led me into content writing. For about a year and a half, I explored storytelling in a different way — learning how to structure thoughts, simplify complexity, and communicate ideas clearly.

In 2019, I found my real calling in project management within a marketing research firm. This is where things truly came together. I had the opportunity to work with global clients like BCG, Dynata, Deloitte, and McKinsey — managing end-to-end projects, handling clients across geographies, and learning how to balance expectations with delivery.

Today, I’m with Simptra Technologies (FYNXT), working in the Forex domain, where I manage implementations, drive cross-team coordination, and ensure that business requirements translate smoothly into execution.

If I had to sum it up — I’m someone who enjoys building things, solving problems, and connecting dots… whether it’s in a new city or in a complex project.
